LATEST AVAILABLE VERSION
==========

Asterism Arcanum Pre-Release 0.1.0:
* Reimagining and Reconfiguring of the alpha version
  * Near complete incompatibility with the previous version, PLEASE BACK UP YOUR WORLD if updating.
  * Mobs:
    * Astromancer - Another school-specific wizard, which spawns in Astral Observatories.
    * Dragonflies - These aggressive predators also spawn in Astral Observatories, and can be used as spell components.
    * Lunar Moths - Spawning on high mountain tops, these insects do not serve a purpose unless summoned, in which case, they can be ridden. Their eggs can be used as throwable weapons and can be farmed using sniffers.
  * Structures:
    * Astral Observatory - Once a center of learning, the observatory now has been overrun by dangerous enemies. It is here that you can find the Astromancer.
  * Items:
    * Astral Armor - This set of armor is crafted the same way as the other school armor sets in Iron's Spells n' Spellbooks, and offers the same benefits.
    * Celestial Staff - This staff is designed to be used by those focusing on the Astral School of magic, and is made through a relatively straightforward recipe. 
    * Astrolabe Spellbook - Although this item is not strictly a book, it carries the same functions as the spellbooks for other schools of magic.
    * Mothspawn - These eggs can occasionally be discovered by sniffers, and are useful both in combat and to create new moths.
    * Lumine and Umbral Lumine - These are decorative blocks that can be found in the Astral Observatory, and crafted.
    * Dragonfly Comb - A block found around Dragonfly spawners, it currently serves as a decorative element for players to use.
  * Spells:
    * Astral Echo: This is a teleportation spell which can be counterspelled, in exchange for its high distance of travel.
    * Brightburst: This is a reimagining of the "Dark Flow" spell from the previous version of the addon. This spell no longer diverts projectiles, but it does force away nearby mobs.
    * Celestial Tether: This is a reimagining of the "Tidal Lock" spell from the previous version of the addon. This version of the spell can absorb multiple attacks before ending, and still allows the caster to remain locked in place, even in the air.
    * Luminous Beam: This spell is a visually improved version of the "Luminous Ray" spell from the previous version of the addon. There is no significant change from the previous version other than appearance-- the spell is still an elongated "cone" style spell, as seen in other spell schools.
    * Piercing Light: This spell generates dozens of small projectiles around the caster, which may damage any nearby enemies.
    * Silvery Barbs: Drawing inspiration from the well known Dungeons and Dragons spell of the same name, this spell can ward off damage to your pets, summons, or allies for a brief moment.
    * Starcutter: This spell may be reminiscent of Fireball or Firecracker. It creates a small orb at the point the caster aims, which quickly explodes and damages nearby entities.
    * Starfire: This spell is identical to the previous version other than visual changes. It serves as a quick-to-cast projectile which can pierce and ricochet.
    * Star Swarm: This is a reimagining of the "Star Swarm" spell from the previous version of the addon. This spell creates barrages of projectiles which fly towards the direction the caster looks, for the duration of the spell.
    * Summon Lunar Moth: This spell summons a flying mount, which moves slowly but will also attempt to defend you from enemies.
  * Unfinished/Inaccessible Features:
    * Astral Sea Dimension: This is an inaccessible dimension which will function similarly to the Pocket Dimension, and will be made accessible in the next major update to this addon.
    * Astral Gateway Spell: This spell can bring you and nearby allies to and from the Astral Sea.
    * Ethereal Talisman: This item is not currently accessible, but has the Astral Gateway Spell imbued into it.
